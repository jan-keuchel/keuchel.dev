# The Bell Curve

## Overview
This work delves into the distribution of human abilities, focusing on intelligence and its societal implications. It sparks debate on how differences shape outcomes.

## Key Points
- **Intelligence Metrics**: Understanding measures of cognitive ability.
- **Social Stratification**: How ability differences influence economic and social structures.
- **Policy Implications**: Debating the role of education and opportunity.

## Discussion Points
- How do individual differences impact societal organization?
- What policies best address varying cognitive abilities?
